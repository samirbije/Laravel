Agrifood Consulting International provides a wide range of consulting and advisory services to governments, development agencies and private companies. Grounded in decades of experience in Food Security and Food Safety, ACI has expanded its expertise to bring leading-edge approaches to policies and practices addressing more inclusive value chain development and climate smart agriculture.

In addition, ACI has significant experience and expertise in working with private companies to encourage investment and collaboratively working with national and sub-national governments and international development agencies.
